A little about me

I'm Ray and since 2018, I’ve been on a journey dedicated to the art of tattooing, with a particular focus on black and gray illustrative realism. My work often explores Japanese themes, intricate weapon designs, and precise geometric detailing. These styles allow me to bring a sense of depth and narrative to each piece, whether it’s a large-scale project or a smaller, more intimate tattoo.

Over the years, I’ve poured my passion into my craft, pushing myself to continuously evolve and improve. Along the way, I’ve been honored to receive some awards, which have been both humbling and motivating. These recognitions inspire me to keep pushing my creative limits, always aiming to bring my clients’ visions to life in a way that is unique and meaningful. Tattooing is more than a profession for me—it’s my way of connecting with people and creating lasting art.
